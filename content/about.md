## Our Mission

NeuroFamilies exists because finding help for your neurodiverse child shouldn't take weeks of searching across dozens of websites.

We centralize the scattered world of autism, ADHD, sensory processing, and neurodiverse resources — so parents spend **minutes** finding what they need, not **weeks**.

## Who We Are

NeuroFamilies was built by a parent raising an autistic child. After years of navigating the maze of therapists, schools, funding programs, and support services, we decided to build the resource we wished existed.

Every listing is scraped from public sources, verified for accuracy, and organized so you can find exactly what you need by location, category, or keyword.

## What We Cover

- **Therapy:** ABA, speech, occupational, physical, mental health
- **Education:** Schools, IEP advocacy, early intervention, tutoring
- **Support:** Respite care, support groups, family counseling
- **Financial:** Grants, Medicaid waivers, ABLE accounts, SSI
- **Recreation:** Camps, adaptive sports, social skills programs
- **Products:** Sensory tools, communication devices, apps
- **Legal:** Special education attorneys, disability rights
- **Adult Services:** Employment, housing, healthcare
- **Crisis:** Emergency resources always available

## Our Principles

1. **Free forever** — Basic search will never be paywalled
2. **Verified listings** — Every resource has a last-checked date
3. **Accessible** — WCAG AA compliant, mobile-first, screen-reader friendly
4. **Independent** — We're not affiliated with any autism organization
5. **Community-driven** — Suggest resources, report errors, share feedback

## Disclaimer

NeuroFamilies provides information about resources and services. We do not endorse, recommend, or guarantee any provider listed on this site. Always verify information directly with providers before making decisions about your family's care.
